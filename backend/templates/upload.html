About FreshShare

Our Mission

FreshShare is a revolutionary platform that bridges the gap between food waste and food insecurity. We use cutting-edge technology to detect food expiry dates and connect donors with local communities, food banks, and individuals in need.

Every day, millions of tons of perfectly edible food are wasted while people go hungry. Our platform creates a sustainable solution by making food redistribution efficient, automated, and impactful.
